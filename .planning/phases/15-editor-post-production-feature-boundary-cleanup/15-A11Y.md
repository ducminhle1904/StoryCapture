## Static audit — minor findings (non-blocking)

1. **`Layers` icon in scene-list header** — missing `aria-hidden="true"`. Cosmetic.
2. **Parse-error chip not in aria-live region** — SR users won't be announced chip appearance. Functional impact: low (scene list remains usable, chip is advisory metadata).
3. **PreviewSurface recording fade-in not reduced-motion-branched** — 200ms opacity-only; low motion-sickness risk.

None of the three are blockers under the Phase 15 exit criteria. All are candidates for a post-Phase-15 a11y polish pass or quick-fix plan.
